How we work

Software that works for you, not on you.

The name's a bit of a wink. The intent isn't: everything NeuroTrocity ships is built on the same four rules.

01 Small on purpose.

One thing, done properly. No feature bloat, no roadmap theatre, no app that grows until it's miserable to use.

02 Honest by default.

No dark patterns, no manipulative nudges, no selling you out. If a choice is good for us and bad for you, we don't ship it.

03 Your data stays yours.

On your device wherever it can be, synced privately through your own iCloud. Never sold. Ever.

04 Made by a human.

A one-person studio in Australia. You email us, a person reads it and replies — no ticket queue, no bot.
