Private by design

Your deals are yours. Full stop.

DisPoint never links to your Qantas, Velocity or Flybuys account, never stores a loyalty password, and never scrapes a rewards site. Your library lives on your device and syncs privately across your own devices with iCloud.

⌂

On-device capture

Screenshot text recognition runs locally. No server of ours ever sees your codes.

⚿

No account linking, ever

A permanent product boundary — not a feature we haven't built yet.
