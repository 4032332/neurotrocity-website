DisPoint — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

DisPoint is a personal tool for saving discount codes and loyalty‑points deals you find yourself, with reminders before they expire. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. In short: DisPoint has no user accounts and no developer‑run servers — the information you save stays on your device and in your own private iCloud.

Information you create

  • The discount codes, points deals, goals, reminder settings, and any notes or screenshots you add are stored on your device using Apple's on‑device database.
  • If you are signed into iCloud, this data syncs across your own Apple devices through Apple's CloudKit (your private iCloud). It is not sent to us and we cannot access it. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • DisPoint does not ask for or store any login or credentials for loyalty programs (Qantas, Velocity, Flybuys, Everyday Rewards, etc.). It never connects to your loyalty accounts.

No account, no analytics

  • DisPoint has no sign‑up or login and collects no name, email, or contact details to run the app. (You can optionally type a display name into the app for a personalised greeting — that name is stored only on your device.)
  • The app contains no analytics, crash‑reporting, or usage‑tracking SDKs.

Advertising

DisPoint's free version shows ads through Google AdMob. Ads are served non‑personalised — the app does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt and does not use your advertising identifier to track you across other apps or websites. To serve and measure ads, Google's ad SDK may process standard technical signals such as a device identifier, coarse (non‑precise) location, and ad‑interaction data, as described in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and Google's AdMob policies. Purchasing DisPoint Pro removes all ads.

Other network requests

  • Deal catalogue. The app downloads a public, curated list of Australian promos from a static file we publish. This is a one‑way download — no information about you is sent.
  • Retailer logos. To show a shop's logo, the app may request a small icon from Google's public favicon service using the retailer's website domain (for example coles.com.au). No personal information is included in these requests.

Purchases

DisPoint Pro is a one‑time in‑app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store. DisPoint does not receive or store your payment details. Purchase handling is covered by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, DisPoint schedules local reminders on your device before a code or deal expires. These are generated on‑device; no push server is used.

Children

DisPoint is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region) and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Your choices

  • Delete any saved item in the app at any time; deleting the app removes its on‑device data from that device.
  • Manage ad and tracking settings in iOS under Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Manage iCloud sync under Settings → [your name] → iCloud.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves; material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
