Support

Common questions. Still stuck? Contact us.

Is DisPoint free?

Yes. The core app — capturing codes and deals, reminders, the calculator, iCloud sync and the widget — is free and ad-supported. A one-time DisPoint Pro purchase removes ads and unlocks extras like unlimited goals, every theme and app icon, CSV export, per-item reminder timing, and the advanced calculator.

How do I capture a deal from a screenshot?

Take a screenshot of the code or offer anywhere on your iPhone, then tap Share and choose DisPoint. The app reads the details on-device and shows you a pre-filled screen to confirm and save. You can also add a deal manually with the + button.

Does DisPoint connect to my Qantas / Velocity / Flybuys account?

No — and it never will. DisPoint doesn't log in to, link, or read any loyalty account, and never stores a loyalty password. Points figures are your own estimates. This is a permanent design choice, for your security.

How does the points estimate work?

The Point Potential calculator multiplies your planned spend by a deal's rate (assuming $1 = 1 point unless you change it) and, for store-rewards deals, applies the fixed conversion to the matching frequent-flyer program. It's an estimate to help you compare deals — not a guarantee of points earned. Always check the offer with the retailer or program.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings → Get DisPoint Pro in the app and complete the one-time purchase. Ads disappear immediately. If you've bought Pro before (or on another device), tap Restore Purchases.

Does my data sync across devices?

If you're signed in to iCloud, your DisPoint library syncs privately across your own Apple devices through iCloud. It's your data in your iCloud — we can't see it.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual items in the app at any time. To remove everything, delete the DisPoint app from your device; to remove synced copies, delete the items in the app or manage the app's data in your iCloud settings. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which devices does DisPoint support?

DisPoint runs on iPhone with iOS 17 or later. It's built for Australian retailers and loyalty programs.

I found a wrong or expired deal in the catalogue.

Thanks — that helps everyone. Let us know and we'll fix or remove it. The curated catalogue is reviewed regularly and stale deals auto-hide at expiry.
