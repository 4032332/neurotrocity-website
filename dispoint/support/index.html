Common questions

  • Do I need an account? No — DisPoint has no sign‑up. Your saved deals stay on your device and sync through your own private iCloud.
  • Does it connect to my Qantas / Velocity / Flybuys / Everyday Rewards account? No. DisPoint never links to loyalty accounts or stores their logins. You update balances yourself.
  • How do reminders work? DisPoint schedules local notifications before a code or deal expires (by default 7 days, 1 day, and the morning of).
  • What does DisPoint Pro include? A one‑time purchase that removes ads and unlocks all themes and icons, unlimited goals, CSV export, per‑item reminders, and the advanced calculator.
